Restaurant Ordering System

Phase 1 flow: customers order from their phone, payment is confirmed at the cashier, then paid orders go to kitchen. Dine-in orders get a table number, takeaway orders get a pickup number.

Recommended flow

  1. Customer opens menu on phone
  2. Customer chooses item and modifiers
  3. System creates temporary order number
  4. Customer goes to cashier to pay
  5. Cashier confirms payment
  6. System assigns table or pickup number
  7. Kitchen receives paid order only
